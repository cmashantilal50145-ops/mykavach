The product

8 shields. Each built from
a real trader's real mistake.

MyKavach does not tell you what to buy or sell. It stops you from doing what you already know you should not do.

1
Stop Loss Gate
Cannot log any trade without entering a stop loss price first. The gate is the feature. You knew you needed one. This makes bypassing it impossible in the heat of the moment.
2
Position Size Enforcer
Every trade shown as % of your capital vs your pre-set safe limit. Displays your personal historical loss rate on oversized trades.
"Bhai, ye trade teri 38% capital le raha hai. 20% se zyada pe tera loss rate 74% hai. Soch le."
3
Revenge Trade Detector
Trade placed within 20 minutes of a loss — flagged instantly with your personal win rate in that window, built from your own history.
"11 minute pehle ₹4,200 gaye. Is waqt tera win rate sirf 12% hai. Ruk ja thoda."
4
Daily Circuit Breaker
Set your daily max loss calmly before 9:15 AM. When hit — trade logging locks for the day. No override in the heat of the moment. The inability to override is the entire feature.
5
Post-Win Warning
After any trade making 2x your average win — mandatory alert before next session. One trader earned ₹1Cr over 3 years and lost ₹95L in one overconfident day. This stops that.
6
Time-of-Day Intelligence
Shows your personal P&L by hour before you trade — built from your own data, not generic advice.
"Tera 9AM win rate: 31%. 12PM win rate: 68%. Abhi 9:22 ho rahi hai — thoda ruk ja."
7
Weekly Behaviour Report
Every Sunday — not P&L, but behaviour. Times you broke your stop loss rule. Cost of rule-breaking in exact rupees. The mirror nobody else holds up for you.
"Is hafte tune 4 baar stop loss rule toda. Un 4 trades mein ₹14,200 gaye. Jab rule follow kiya tab average ₹340 ka profit tha."
8
Accountability Partner
One trusted person gets a WhatsApp notification when your circuit breaker trips. No amounts — just the signal. Because isolation is the trader's worst enemy.
